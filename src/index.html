We are an inclusive open AI research platform empowering students and folks to learn and collaborate with each other in AI R&D. Here's what we do.

Writing Research Paper

We come up with new research ideas and collaborate with different universities, brainstorm the idea to finally come up with some novel solution and publish that in potential top journals or conferences.

Taking sessions

Community members either present their own published paper or Other's published paper, to make other interested folks to get aware with latest deep learning techniques. Members also try to provide tutorial sessions on other popular and latest deep learning topics as well.

Open source

Collaborating with newer people and taking part to several major open source contributions like Hugging face contributions through kaggle competitions is another interesting thing that CORD.ai highly promotes.
